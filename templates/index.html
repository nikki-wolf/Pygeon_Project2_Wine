Wine History

By Pygeons 2.0, v.1.0

Parallel coordinate plot visualizes the multivariate wine dataset. To show the interrelation between wine rating, price, variety, and subvariety on a country basis comporising a 5-dimensional space, a polyline with vertices on the country, wine rating , wine price, and wine variety, and wine suvarietyis plotted.
User can drage the lines along the axes to filter or drag axis names acroos the plot to rearrange variables.

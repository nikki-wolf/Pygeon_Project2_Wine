Parallel coordinate plot visualizes the multivariate belly-button sample dataset. To show the total number of bacteria/Archaea of each sample in 5-dimensional space, a polyline with vertices on the sample number,age , gender, and frequency of weekly scrubs is plotted. The total number of bacteria/Archaea found on that sample is represented by the color of te polyline (colorbar).
User can drage the lines along the axes to filter age and scrub/week or drag axis names acroos the plot to rearrange variables.
For examople, the screenshot filters the samples with age between 20-50 yrs and 1-3 scrubs/week for both male and female genders. It also demonstrates that sample #946 has ~2500 bacteria/Archaea found on his/her belly-button (highlighted in dark red color)
